How I Work

Principles I ship by

01
End-to-end, not just notebooks
A model that never leaves a .ipynb isn't done. I build for deployment, monitoring, and retraining from day one.
02
Data first, model second
Most wins come from feature engineering and clean pipelines — not from swapping XGBoost for something fancier.
03
Automate the boring parts
Scraping, ETL, spreadsheet ops — if I do it twice, I script it. Agents and RPA should serve humans, not distract them.
04
Honest uncertainty
Calibrated confidence beats bold predictions. I surface model limitations instead of hiding them behind polish.
